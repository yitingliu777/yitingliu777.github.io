Biography

Yiting Liu is a postdoctoral scholar at UCSD, working under the supervision of Prof. Andrew B. Kahng. She received the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Fudan University in 2024, under the supervision of Prof. Li Shang. She completed her M.S. in computer science and technology from Beijing Institute of Technology in 2020 and her B.S. in computer science and technology from Northwest A&F University in 2017.

Her research interests include electronic design automation and VLSI physical design
