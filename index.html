Biography

Yiting Liu is a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Fudan University, under the supervision of Prof. Li Shang. She received the B.S. degree in computer science and technology from Northwest A&F University in 2017 and the M.S. degree in computer science and technology from Beijing Institute of Technology in 2020.

Her research interests include electronic design automation, VLSI physical design via deep learning and data privacy protection.
